Getting Started

Fitrio is a fitness app based around short, focused workout routines that you can do at home. The only equipment you'll need is a pull-up bar.

A Fitrio routine is made up of just three exercises. The beginner routine, for example, is one chin-up, two push-ups, and three squats. The number of times you do each exercise is called the 'reps' (or repetitions) and doing the whole sequence is called a 'set'.

Here's how it works:

1. Warm up for five minutes.

1. Warm up for five minutes.

You can do anything you like: jog on the spot while waving your arms around, go through the routine exercises very slowly, or follow along with a warm up video on youtube. Anything to loosen up your muscles and get your heart beating faster.

2. Do the three routine exercises for 20 minutes.

2. Do the three routine exercises for 20 minutes.

Set a timer so you know when the 20 minutes are up. Try not to rush the movements and concentrate on keeping your form as good as you can. Count every set you complete.

When you get tired, take a break, but don't stop the timer. The idea is to complete as many sets as you can in the 20 minutes. As your fitness improves, you'll need fewer breaks and your scores will get better and better.

3. Cool down for five minutes.

3. Cool down for five minutes.

After your 20 minutes are up, it's a good idea to spend five minutes doing some gentle exercise and stretching, to cool down. After that, record the number of sets you finished on the Fitrio app and try to beat your score next time!

Here's how to log your workouts:

Click the “Add Log” button beneath the routine you’ve worked through on the My Routines page. There’s also a button at the top of the Workout Log page.

Enter the details of your workout. For ‘Sets Completed’, remember to only include sets you finished inside the 20 minutes - if you were halfway through one, it doesn’t count!

Push the green ‘Add Log’ button and you’re done! Once you’ve recorded a few workouts, you can see your personal best score and a graph of your progress by clicking the ‘Track’ button on the My Routines page.

Still have questions? Check out our faq section, where you can find full instructions on how to perform the exercises in the beginner and intermediate routines as well as answers to other common questions.
